Products

Since 1969, we've been providing groomers with the highest quality tools
for every day use. We've tested thousands of grooming products in high-
volume grooming shop environments to insure that we only offer the best,
most durable products in each category. We also understand that many
groomers have tight budgets so we offer the widest variety of products at
the lowest prices you'll find anywhere - we guarantee it!

Dog Clippers

The clipper is the workhorse tool for all groomers and we stock the
largest inventory of clippers available anywhere! So whether you
prefer the power of a Single Speed Clipper or the versatility of
2-Speed or Variable Speed Clipper we carry the complete lines of the
leading manufactures - Andis®, Oster®, Laube®, Wahl® and
Conair®. We also stock an excellent selection of cordless dog
clippers and trimmers for specialized jobs.

Clipper Blades

Groomers have become more demanding on the quality of their
clipper blades, they need them to stay sharper longer. We offer the
most complete selection of clipper blades on the planet! So if you are
looking for the latest Titanium & Ceramic clipper blades that stay
sharper 10 times longer and 80% cooler than standard blades or you
are prefer the economical steel blade, you'll find it here. We offer
clipper blades from all of the leading manufactures - Andis, Oster,
Laube, Wahl, and Conair.

Grooming Tables

We stock a complete line of grooming tables that come in an
assortment of heights and lengths. Choose from economical plywood
top grooming portable tables to the top-of-the-line Hydraulic tables.
Whatever your needs are you're sure to find the perfect table for your
grooming shop at a price that allows you to maximize your
investment.
